Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace was the woman who pioneered the field of computer science. She created a sequence of how to calculate Bernoulli numbers which is known to be the first computer program.

Ms. Lovelace illustrates the brilliance of women. In the modern age, the STEM field underrepresents womxn of color. The womxn on this website are modern day Adas, they are pioneering a new diverse future for computer science.

The resilience of womxn of color in tech

Imposter syndrome, systemic barriers, discrimination, prejudices...the list goes on. Womxn of color fall victim to countless adversities throughout their lives, yet they still perservere and pioneer pathways in the world of technology.

I collected stories from various womxn of color in tech with the hopes of inspiring you and your endeavors as well as validating any adversity you have or are currently experiencing. Most importantly, let these stories remind you, we are stronger together.
