Women and non-binary folks of color taking on the tech world, Ada style.


Ada Lovelace pioneered the field of computer science. Her sequence for calculating Bernoulli numbers is recognized as the first computer program, which makes the first programmer... a woman!

Ms. Lovelace illuminates the brilliance of women and non-binary folks. The STEM field severely underrepresents women and non-binary folks of color. This website celebrates modern day Adas, women and non-binary folks who are pioneering a new, diverse future for the tech world.

The resilience of women and non-binary folks of color in tech

Imposter syndrome, systemic barriers, discrimination, biases...the list goes on. Women and non-binary folks of color must brave countless adversities throughout their education, careers, and lives. Yet, they are resilient and still persevere to pioneer pathways in the world of technology.

We are Stronger Together

I have collected stories from various women of color in tech (and am still looking to collect stories from non-binary folks!) with the hopes of inspiring you and validating any adversity you have or are currently experiencing. Most importantly, let these stories remind you, you are not alone.
