Who was Ada?

The resilience of womxn of color in tech

Imposter syndrome, systemic barriers, discrimination, prejudices...the list goes on. Women and non-binary students of color fall victim to countless adversities throughout their lives yet they still perservere and pioneer pathways in the world of technology. I collected stories of resilience from various women and non-binary people of color in tech with the hopes of inspiring you and your endeavors as well as validating any adversity you have or are currently experiencing. Most importantly, let these stories remind you, we are stronger together.
