Ada Lovelace pioneered the field of computer science. Her sequence for calculating Bernoulli numbers is recognized as the first computer program, which makes...the first computer programmer a woman!

Ms. Lovelace illustrates the brilliance of women and non-binary folks. The STEM field underrepresents women and non-binary folks of color. This website highlights modern day Adas, women and non-binary folks who are pioneering a new diverse future for computer science.

The resilience of women and non-binary folks of color in tech

Imposter syndrome, systemic barriers, discrimination, prejudices...the list goes on. Women and non-binary folks of color fall victim to countless adversities throughout their lives, yet they still perservere and pioneer pathways in the world of technology.

I collected stories from various women of color in tech (and still looking to collect stories from non-binary folks!) with the hopes of inspiring you and your endeavors as well as validating any adversity you have or are currently experiencing. Most importantly, let these stories remind you, we are stronger together.
